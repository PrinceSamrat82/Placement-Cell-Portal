Acadmic Facilities

Katihar Engineering College has a computer centre with 250+ computers, equipped with top-notch processors and hardware. The system includes various software from various engineering fields, providing excellent facilities for students and professors.
The centre also features a smart platform for studies, a projector, and various labs such as MATLAB, High-level Programming lab, Assembly Language Programming lab, Linux OS implementation, CAD, CAM, DBMS, Language Lab, and competitive programming for student development.
Wi-Fi is available in all academic buildings, libraries, administrative blocks, residential buildings, and hostels. All college members can access the computer systems, and the resources are efficiently used to provide knowledge in various fields.
Regular presentations, exams, classes, and lab classes are organized to provide the best education to students.

Departments & Programs at KEC Katihar

Katihar Engineering College offers a comprehensive education in civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electronics engineering (VLSI Design And Technology), computer science & engineering, electrical and electronics engineering, applied science & humanities, and food processing.
Each department focuses on different aspects of engineering, such as infrastructure projects, mechanical systems, electronics, computer science & engineering, applied science & humanities, and food processing. Students learn about structural analysis, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, environmental engineering, software development, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, and computer networks.
The college also provides a solid foundation in basic sciences and humanities for understanding and applying engineering principles. For more information, students can visit the college's official website or contact the college directly.

Recriutment Process

Our process has evolved over year to ensure that our recruiters have a seamless hiring experience. Here we have simplified the steps for you.

1.The Placement Office appoints a point of contact for you and sends invitations to recruiting organizations

2.Recruiters interested in hiring will create their online account on Link

3.Recruiters can now create jobs/internships containing the details of opportunity as required by the Placement Office

4.The details of the job are verified by the executive officers. (Remuneration details and any other ambiguous information)

5.After successful verification, the job is made available online to the students, as per dates decided by the Placement Office

6.Interested students show their willingness to appear for the recruitment process of a company by applying for its Job

7.Companies can view resumes of interested students and shortlist students using their online account.

8.Companies can proceed with their tests/screening process after finalizing the schedule in coordinance with Placement Office

9.Companies shortlist the selected students for final interview process

10.Placement Office allots dates to organizations for campus interviews based on various details given by companies

11.The organization confirms the dates with the Placement Office

12.Organizations provide the list of students, they are extending offers to at the end of their interview slot

13.Placement Office notifies an organization with the acceptance of selected students

14.Organization generate and delivers the offer letter to the selected candidates consistent with the information of the job created online
